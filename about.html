About Me

My interest in coding and web development began the summer of 2022 as I was searching for a career which balanced structure and creativity. I discovered the SheCodes workshop through a friend and was immediately intrigued by web development, coding language, and front-end design. Though still in my current role, I began the SheCodes workshops at the end of August 2022 and I seek to become a full-time developer soon after completing the course!

In my free time, you can find me playing guitar, reading, sipping on coffee or wine, enoying the outdoors, cuddled up with my two cats, or travelling around the world with my partner and taking pictures as I go.
